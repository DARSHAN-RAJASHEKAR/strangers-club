House
Rules.

Strangers Club is a quiet place. These are the things that keep it that way.

I
Real people only.

Your account is you. No bots, no fake identities, no shared logins.

II
You vouch for who you invite.

Every member was let in by someone. When you send an invitation, you're saying this person belongs here. Their conduct reflects on you.

III
Be good to each other.

No harassment, no hate, no cruelty. Disagreement is fine. Making someone feel unwelcome is not.

IV
No promotion. No spam.

This is not a broadcast channel. Don't pitch products, drive traffic, or mass-message people who didn't ask.

V
What's shared here stays here.

Respect the privacy of conversations. Don't screenshot or share messages outside the club without the sender's consent.

VI
Break the rules, lose your seat.

Membership is a privilege, not a right. We reserve the right to remove anyone who makes this place worse for everyone else.
